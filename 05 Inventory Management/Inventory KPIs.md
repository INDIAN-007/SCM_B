# Inventory KPIs

> **Source:** Kuldeepak Ch.4
> **Tags:** #inventory #kpi #metrics

---

## Quality Indicators

| KPI | Formula | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Inventory Accuracy Rate** | System stock = Physical stock? (%) | WMS/BIN card vs actual count |
| **Stock Wastage Ratio** | Unusable stock / Total stock × 100 | % lost to expiry or damage |
| **Stock-Out Rate** | SKUs with stockout / Total SKUs × 100 | Frequency of availability failures |
| **Order Fill Rate** | Orders fully fulfilled / Total orders × 100 | % orders delivered complete |

---

## Responsiveness Indicators

| KPI | Formula | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Order Lead Time** | Time from order receipt to fulfilment | End-to-end order cycle in facility |
| **Order TAT** | Pick + Pack + Load + Documentation time | Internal processing speed |

---

## Financial Indicators

| KPI | Formula | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Inventory Holding Cost** | Rentals + Utilities + Insurance + Labour + Damage + Admin | Total annual cost to hold inventory |
| **Unaccounted / Lost Stock Cost** | Value of missing or unaccounted stock | Loss visibility; drives replenishment decisions |
| **Order Fulfilment Cost** | Total fulfilment cost / Orders | Per-order cost including labour, equipment, consumables |

---

## Productivity Indicators

| KPI | Formula | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Inventory Turns** | COGS / Average Inventory | How many times inventory cycles per year |
| **Days Inventory Outstanding** | 365 / Inventory Turns | How many days of demand are covered by avg inventory |
| **Cycle Count Compliance** | Counts done / Counts scheduled × 100 | Adherence to counting plan (perpetual or periodic) |

## Benchmark Context

| Industry | Inventory Turns (approximate) |
|---|---|

| Consumer electronics | 8–15 turns |
