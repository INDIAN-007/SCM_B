# TIMWOODS — The 8 Wastes of Lean

> **Source:** Kuldeepak Ch.1 & Ch.6
> **Tags:** #lean #waste #timwoods

---

## Overview

TIMWOODS (also recalled as **DOWNTIME**) represents the 8 types of waste in supply chain and manufacturing. Any activity or process that does not add value to the customer is waste.

> "Wastages and inefficient supply chain may lead to disastrous outcomes." — Kuldeepak Singh

---

## The 8 Wastes

| Letter | Waste | Supply Chain Example | Inefficiency Generated |
|---|---|---|---|
| **T** | **Transportation** | Unnecessary movement of goods between locations | Damage during transit; fuel cost; time lost |
| **I** | **Inventory** | Excess raw materials, WIP, finished goods | Capital tied up; obsolescence; storage cost |
| **M** | **Motion** | Unnecessary movement of people in a warehouse | Injury risk; fatigue; productivity loss |
| **W** | **Waiting** | Idle time at production, dock, or decision point | Lost productive hours; delayed orders |
| **O** | **Overproduction** | Producing beyond customer demand | Additional storage, transport, and handling |
| **O** | **Overprocessing** | More features or steps than customer needs | Wasted effort and material |
| **D** | **Defects** | Quality failures requiring rework or scrap | Extra cost; rework labour; dissatisfied customers |
| **S** | **Skills (Non-utilized talent)** | Wrong person for the task; skills not leveraged | Demotivation; high cost with low productivity |

---

## Application in Supply Chain

| Area | Common Wastes |
|---|---|
| **Warehouse** | Motion (poor layout), Waiting (dock congestion), Defects (picking errors) |
| **Transport** | Transportation waste (inefficient routes), Waiting (loading delays) |
| **Manufacturing** | Overproduction (forecast error), Inventory (WIP build-up), Defects |
| **Procurement** | Overprocessing (excessive approvals), Waiting (supplier delays), Skills |
| **Planning** | Overproduction (over-forecasting), Inventory (wrong mix) |

## Waste Reduction Tools

| Tool | Primary Wastes Addressed |
|---|---|
| Kanban / JIT | Overproduction, Inventory, Waiting |
| 5S / 6S | Motion, Defects, Waiting |
| SMED | Waiting, Overproduction |
| Poka-Yoke | Defects |
| Kaizen | All 8 wastes |
| Value Stream Mapping | All — visualises entire waste picture |
