## KPI Framework — Four Categories

### Quality Indicators (How well?)
| KPI | Formula | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Perfect Execution %** | (Perfect consignments / Total) × 100 | Shipments reaching destination without damage, loss, or delay |
| **On-Time / Before-Time Delivery %** | (On-time shipments / Total) × 100 | Lead time compliance rate |
| **Total Travel Between Accidents** | km / Number of accidents | Safety indicator — identifies problem routes or drivers |

### Responsiveness Indicators (How fast?)
| KPI | Description |
|---|---|
| **Avg Loading/Unloading Time** | Time per shipment to load or unload; drill by warehouse, route, product type |
| **Average Lead Time** | Avg transit/delivery time from shipment to endpoint; benchmark annually |
| **Vehicle Turnaround Time** | Idle time between vehicle arrival and departure at facility — measures internal process speed |

### Financial / Cost Indicators (How much?)
| KPI | Formula | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Avg Transport Cost/km** | Total cost / Total km | Benchmark for rate negotiation with 3PL/4PL |
| **Cost-to-Sales Ratio** | Transport cost / Net sales × 100 | Budget and benchmarking tool |
| **Fuel vs Vehicle Running Cost** | Fuel cost / Total vehicle running cost × 100 | Tracks fuel efficiency; useful for owned/leased fleet |

### Productivity Indicators (How efficiently?)
| KPI | Description |
|---|---|
| **Vehicle Capacity Utilisation** | Actual load / Rated capacity — by vehicle type, route, primary/secondary |
| **Route Planning Efficiency** | Avg stops per km for LTL — higher = better (circular route optimisation) |
| **Vehicle Uptime %** | % time vehicle is available and operational — critical for leased fleets |

---

## Author's Tip (Kuldeepak)

> Use **North West Corner Rule, Least Cost Method, or Vogel's Approximation Method** (or software) for scientific route optimisation. Measuring stops per km is the output metric.
