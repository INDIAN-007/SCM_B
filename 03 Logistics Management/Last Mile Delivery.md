# Last Mile Delivery

> **Source:** Chopra Ch.14 · Kuldeepak Ch.2
> **Tags:** #logistics #lastmile #ecommerce

---

## Definition

Last mile delivery is the final step of the delivery process — from a distribution centre or hub to the end customer's door. It is typically the **most expensive and time-consuming** part of the supply chain.

---

## Why It's Critical

- Customer satisfaction depends heavily on last-mile experience
- ~28–53% of total delivery cost is in the last mile
- E-commerce growth has intensified last-mile expectations (Amazon Effect)
- Urban congestion, security, and returns add complexity

---

## Last-Mile Delivery Models

| Model | Description | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Courier delivery** | Standard carrier to home/office | FedEx, UPS, Delhivery |
| **Milk run** | One vehicle visits multiple stops in sequence | B2B retail replenishment |
| **Click-and-collect** | Customer picks up at store or locker | Amazon Lockers, Kirana stores |
| **Same-day delivery** | Delivered on day of order | Amazon Prime Now, Swiggy Instamart |
| **Drone / autonomous** | Emerging technology for remote areas | Amazon Prime Air pilots |
| **Crowdsourced** | Gig workers for last leg | Dunzo, Porter |

---

## Mumbai Dabbawalas (Chopra) — Case Study

- 5,000 workers deliver 200,000 lunches daily across Mumbai
- Zero technology-based tracking — purely process-driven
- Error rate: 1 in 6 million deliveries (Six Sigma level)
- Pure pull system: each dabbawala knows exactly what to collect and deliver
- Lesson: **Process excellence, not technology, is the foundation of last-mile reliability**

---

## Amazon Effect (Kuldeepak)

Modern customers now expect:
- Agile, flexible, responsive delivery
- At lower cost
- With real-time tracking

Traditional organisations are forced to either ramp up technologically or leverage e-commerce supply chains.

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Description |
|---|---|
| On-time delivery % | % shipments delivered on or before promised date |
| Cost per delivery | Total last-mile cost / deliveries made |
| First-attempt delivery rate | % deliveries successful on first attempt |
| Returns rate | % deliveries returned |
| Customer satisfaction (CSAT) | Delivery experience rating |
