# E-commerce & Quick Commerce — SCM Overview

> **Tags:** #industry #ecommerce #quickcommerce #lastmile #SCM

---

## Industry Profile

| Attribute | Characteristic |
|---|---|
| **Demand Pattern** | 24/7, highly variable, event-driven (sales, flash deals) |
| **Fulfilment Model** | Warehouse → Sortation Centre → Last-mile delivery |
| **Key Challenge** | Last-mile cost (50–60% of total logistics cost), returns management |
| **Speed Promise** | Same-day to 10-minute delivery (quick commerce) |
| **Returns Rate** | 20–40% (fashion); 5–10% (electronics); 2–5% (FMCG) |
| **Companies** | Amazon, Flipkart, Zepto, Blinkit, Shopee, Lazada, Zalora |

---

## Core Methods

| Method | Application |
|---|---|
| **Fulfilment Centre (FC) Design** | Optimised for individual order picking; not pallet movement |
| **Dark Store** | Small format warehouse in city; enables 10–30 min delivery |
| **Zone Picking + Batch Picking** | Pickers assigned zones; multiple orders picked per trip |
| **Slotting Optimisation** | High-velocity items placed near packing stations; reduces travel time |
| **Returns Processing** | Reverse logistics: quality check → restock vs liquidate vs dispose |
| **Demand Sensing** | Real-time sales data → dynamic replenishment to FCs |
| **Surge Pricing / Dynamic Pricing** | Adjust prices during peak demand to manage demand |

---

## Quick Commerce (Q-Commerce) Model

- Promise: 10–30 minute delivery from order
- Infrastructure: **Dark stores** every 2–3 km in dense urban areas
- SKU range: Limited (2,000–5,000 high-velocity items only)
- Inventory: 1–3 days cover; replenished from hub daily
- **Critical KPIs:** Promise fulfilment %, Out-of-stock at dark store %, Rider utilisation %

## Last-Mile Models

| Model | Speed | Cost | Scalability |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Own fleet** | High control | High fixed cost | Limited |
| **Gig delivery (3PL)** | Flexible | Variable | High |
| **Locker/PUDO pickup** | Slower for customer | Low | Very high |
| **Hyperlocal (same building)** | Fastest | Very low | Limited geography |

## E-commerce KPIs

| KPI | Formula | Target |
|---|---|---|
| **Perfect Order Rate** | Orders with no error / Total orders × 100 | > 95% |
| **On-Time Delivery %** | Delivered on/before promise / Total orders × 100 | > 95% |
| **Returns Rate %** | Returns / Total orders × 100 | < 10% (general); < 5% (FMCG) |
| **FC Picking Accuracy %** | Correctly picked / Total lines × 100 | > 99.5% |
| **Last-Mile Cost per Order** | Total last-mile cost / Orders delivered | Minimise; < 15% of order value |
| **Out-of-Stock Rate at FC** | SKUs stocked out / Total SKUs × 100 | < 2% |
| **Returns Processing Time** | Time from return initiation to restock | < 3 days |

---

## Tips

1. **Inventory positioning is everything** — wrong FC has the item; customer is elsewhere → split shipment or delay
2. **Seasonal readiness** — stock up FCs 2 weeks before sales events (Big Billion Day, 11.11)
3. **Returns are a profit centre** — fast returns processing = faster restock = lower lost sales
4. **Slotting is never done** — review FC slotting monthly; product velocities change constantly
5. **Carrier diversification** — never depend on one last-mile partner; maintain 2–3 for redundancy
6. **Data is your inventory manager** — real-time POS/cart data should drive replenishment, not weekly reports
