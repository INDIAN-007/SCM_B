## Quick Formula Lookup
| Metric | Formula |
|---|---|
| Supply Chain Surplus | Customer Value − Supply Chain Cost |
| EOQ | √(2DS / H) |
| Safety Stock | Z × σ_demand × √Lead Time |
| Reorder Point | (Avg Demand × Lead Time) + Safety Stock |
| Inventory Turns | COGS / Avg Inventory |
| C2C Cycle | Inventory Days + Receivable Days − Payable Days |
| GMROI | Gross Profit / Avg Inventory Cost × 100 |
| Turn-Earn Index | Inventory Turns × Gross Profit % × 100 |
| Perfect Order Rate | (Total Orders − Erratic Orders) / Total Orders × 100 |
| TSCC | Material + Manpower + Inv. Holding + Logistics + Overheads |
