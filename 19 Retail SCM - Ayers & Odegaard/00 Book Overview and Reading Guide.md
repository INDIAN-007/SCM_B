## What Makes This Book Unique

Unlike Chopra (academic, all industries) and Kuldeepak (practitioner, India-focused), this book is:
- **Retail-specific** — every concept is grounded in retail and consumer goods
- **Multi-echelon** — covers the full chain from second-tier supplier to end customer
- **Financially anchored** — ties every SCM decision to income statement and balance sheet impact
- **Collaboration-heavy** — dedicated chapters on multi-company collaboration and partner management

| Framework | Chapter | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Retail SC Reference Model** | Ch 1 | 7-layer SC from second-tier supplier to customer |
| **DuPont Strategic Profit Model** | Ch 2 | Links inventory turns and margin to ROE |
| **Merchandise Budget** | Ch 2 | Planning tool for staple and fashion buying |
