---

## Why KPIs Matter

In a VUCA (Volatile, Uncertain, Complex, Ambiguous) world, supply chain leaders need measurable, time-saving, easy-to-interpret indicators with **actionable insights** for decision-making.

---

## Kuldeepak's PCS + TGT Framework

### Quality / Service Indicators (PCS)
| KPI | What It Measures |
|---|---|
| **P** — Perfect Order Management | Error-free rate at every stage of purchase order |
| **C** — Cash-to-Cash Cycle Time | How long operating capital is tied up |
| **S** — Supply Chain Cycle Time | Overall efficiency of the supply chain |

### Cost Indicators (TGT)
| KPI | What It Measures |
|---|---|
| **T** — Turn-Earn Index | Balance between inventory turns and gross profit |
| **G** — GMROI | Gross profit earned per rupee invested in inventory |
| **T** — Total Supply Chain Cost | End-to-end cost from material to delivery |

---

## Four Measurement Categories (Kuldeepak)

| Category | Question | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| **Quality** | How well was the task performed? | Picking accuracy, on-time delivery, perfect order % |
| **Response Time** | How long did it take? | Lead time, order processing time, TAT |
| **Financial / Cost** | How much did it cost? | Transport cost/km, holding cost, GMROI |
| **Productivity** | How effectively were resources used? | Units/hour, vehicle utilisation, inventory turns |

## Chopra's Financial Metrics (Ch.3)

| Metric | Formula |
|---|---|
| ROE | Net Income / Shareholder Equity |
| ROA | Net Income / Total Assets |
| Profit Margin | Net Income / Revenue |
| Asset Turnover | Revenue / Total Assets |
| Inventory Turns (INVT) | COGS / Average Inventory |
| ART (Accounts Receivable Turns) | Revenue / Accounts Receivable |
| APT (Accounts Payable Turns) | COGS / Accounts Payable |
| PPET (PP&E Turns) | Revenue / PP&E |
| C2C Cycle | DIO + DSO − DPO |

## Benchmark Comparison (Chopra — 2013 Data)

| Metric | Amazon | Nordstrom |
|---|---|---|
| ROE | 2.81% | 38.42% |
| ROA | 0.91% | 10.37% |
| Profit Margin | 0.49% | 6.91% |
| Asset Turnover | 1.85 | 1.50 |
| Inventory Turns | 7.31 | 5.46 |
| C2C Cycle | −10.53 weeks | positive |

*Amazon's negative C2C means it collects revenue 10+ weeks before paying suppliers — a massive working capital advantage.*
