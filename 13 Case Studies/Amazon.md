# Amazon — Supply Chain Case Study

---

## Overview

Amazon is arguably the world's most sophisticated supply chain organisation — it turned SC excellence into a **primary competitive advantage** and revenue source (AWS, FBA).

---

## Key Supply Chain Innovations

| Innovation | Description |
|---|---|
| **Fulfilment Centre network** | Strategically located FCs near major population centres — enables fast delivery |
| **Algorithmic inventory positioning** | AI determines which SKUs to stock at which FC based on demand prediction |
| **Amazon Kiva robots** | Robotic pods bring shelves to human pickers — 3x productivity improvement |
| **Predictive stocking** | Anticipatory shipping — product moves toward customer before order placed |
| **Prime delivery ecosystem** | 2-day, 1-day, same-day delivery creates lock-in effect |
| **Last-mile innovation** | Amazon Flex (gig drivers), Amazon delivery stations, Prime Now hubs |
| **Cross-docking** | High-velocity products transferred without storage |
| **Control tower** | Real-time visibility across entire network |
| **FBA (Fulfilled by Amazon)** | Third-party sellers use Amazon SC infrastructure |

---

## Financial Impact of SC Excellence

| Year | Context |
|---|---|
| 2013 | INVT = 7.31, C2C = −10.53 weeks, ART = 15.62 |
| 2015 | Outbound shipping cost: $11.54B; net shipping loss 19x net income |
| Goal | Every dollar saved in outbound shipping = massive profit improvement |

---

## Amazon Effect on Supply Chain Industry

"The Amazon Effect" — customer expectations now include:
- Next-day (or same-day) delivery
- Free returns
- Real-time tracking
- Seamless omnichannel experience

This has forced every retailer and B2B company to rethink their SC capabilities.

---

## SC Strategy Elements

| Driver | Amazon's Approach |
|---|---|
| Facilities | Dense FC network close to population |
| Inventory | Data-driven positioning; FBA reduces owned inventory |
| Transportation | Own delivery stations + carriers + Prime Air drones |
| Information | Real-time visibility across all SC stages |
| Sourcing | Diverse supplier base; own private labels |
| Pricing | Dynamic pricing algorithms |

---

## Lessons

1. Supply chain IS the competitive advantage — not an afterthought
2. Data and technology are force multipliers in SC
3. Negative C2C is achievable and highly valuable
4. Customer expectations set by the best become the new baseline for everyone
