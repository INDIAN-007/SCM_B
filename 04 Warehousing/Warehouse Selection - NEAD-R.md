# Warehouse Selection — NEAD-R Framework

> **Source:** Kuldeepak Ch.3

---

## Why It Matters

Warehouse selection is an iterative, exhaustive process with major investment implications. Poor selection leads to long-term inefficiency and cost burden.

> The Indian warehousing sector is projected to see huge investments in integrated warehousing in the coming years.

---

## NEAD-R Process (Kuldeepak)

*Author's Tip: Remember NEAD-R (pronounced "NIDAR" — fearless) for judiciously taking this decision.*

| Step | Name | Key Activities |
|---|---|---|
| **N** | Need Assessment | Define throughput requirements, SKU types, volume, temperature needs, value-added services |
| **E** | Evaluate Options | Survey available locations; shortlist based on infrastructure, connectivity, labour market |
| **A** | Analyse | Cost-benefit analysis (build, lease, contract); proximity to customers and suppliers; zoning compliance |
| **D** | Decide | Final selection based on weighted scoring of factors |
| **R** | Review | Post-implementation review; continuous improvement |

---

## Key Selection Factors

| Factor | Considerations |
|---|---|
| **Location** | Proximity to customers, suppliers, ports, highways, airports |
| **Infrastructure** | Road access, power, water, drainage, internet connectivity |
| **Space & Layout** | Floor area, ceiling height, dock doors, column spacing |
| **Labour** | Availability, skill level, cost, unionisation |
| **Compliance** | Zoning, fire safety, environmental, food safety (FSSAI), pharma (WHO-GMP) |
| **Cost** | Rent/purchase, utilities, insurance, taxes |
| **Scalability** | Can the space expand as business grows? |
| **Technology** | WMS readiness, RFID/barcode infrastructure |

---

## Build vs Lease vs Contract Decision

| Option | Pros | Cons |
|---|---|---|
| **Own / Build** | Full control, no rent escalation, asset on balance sheet | High capex, inflexible |
| **Lease (long-term)** | Lower upfront, predictable cost | Less flexible than short-term |
| **3PL / Contract** | Variable cost, expert operation, rapid setup | Less control, dependency |
