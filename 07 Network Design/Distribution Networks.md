## Factors Affecting Distribution Network Design

### Customer Needs
- Response time
- Product variety
- Product availability
- Customer experience
- Order visibility (tracking)
- Returnability (reverse logistics)

### Supply Chain Costs
- Inventory cost
- Transportation cost
- Facility cost
- Information cost

**Key insight:** Faster response requires inventory closer to customer → higher inventory cost. The optimal network balances these.

---

## Six Design Options for Distribution Networks

| Model | Flow | Inventory Location | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Manufacturer Storage + Direct Shipping** | Mfr → Customer | At manufacturer | High variety, low demand, large orders |
| **Manufacturer Storage + Carrier Aggregation (Merge-in-Transit)** | Mfr → Merge point → Customer | At manufacturer | Multi-item orders from different sources |
| **Distributor Storage + Package Carrier Delivery** | Mfr → DC → Customer | At distributor/DC | Moderate variety, moderate demand |
| **Distributor Storage + Last-Mile Delivery** | Mfr → DC → Customer home | At DC | High-value, service-intensive delivery |
| **Manufacturer/Distributor Storage + Customer Pickup** | Mfr/DC → Pickup point | At mfr/DC | Customer prefers control, cost-sensitive |
| **Retail Storage + Customer Pickup** | Mfr → DC → Store → Customer | At store | Fast response, high turns, standard products |

---

## Comparing Network Options

| Factor | Retailer | DC + Carrier | Mfr Direct | Online |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Response time | Immediate | 1–2 days | 1–2 weeks | 1–7 days |
| Inventory cost | High (many locations) | Medium | Low | Low |
| Transport cost | Low (bulk inbound) | Medium | High (direct ship) | Medium–High |
| Variety | Low | Medium | Very high | Very high |
| Infrastructure | High | Medium | Low (fewer facilities) | Low |

## Omni-Channel Considerations (Chopra Ch.4)

Modern retail requires serving customers across:
- Physical stores
- E-commerce websites
- Mobile apps
- Click-and-collect (BOPIS — Buy Online, Pick Up In Store)
- Same-day delivery

Each channel has different SC requirements — the network must flex to serve all simultaneously.

**Challenge:** The SC that is most efficient for brick-and-mortar is often not the most efficient for online — forcing firms to run hybrid networks.
